Gravity Plinko (2026)

Game designer & programmer · Solo Unity prototype
3–5 minute runs

A 3D Plinko‑inspired prototype about choosing risk levels and reading probabilities, tuned for “one more run” sessions.

  • Designed the core loop (drop → bounce → reward → spend → repeat).
  • Iterated peg layouts and payout curves to balance fairness vs. excitement.
  • Implemented Unity systems for spawning, scoring, and simple economy feedback.

Step Runner (2026)

Game designer · Mobile prototype
Daily‑session loop

A walking‑powered progression system where real‑world steps become in‑game currency for unlocking routes and upgrades.

  • Designed the economy so small daily play still feels rewarding.
  • Explored different sinks and upgrades to avoid runaway inflation.

About

Who am I?

I'm an extroverted guy who enjoys cycling, trying unconventional things, and living life to the fullest. I believe we should live in a way that, at the end of our journey, there are no regrets about the things we wished we had done. My passion is embracing life completely and being my authentic self with everyone, anywhere.

Most of my work is built in Unity (HDRP/URP) with C#, and I often handle both design and basic implementation so I can iterate quickly on ideas. I’m especially interested in systems design, level flow, and UX for moment‑to‑moment gameplay.
